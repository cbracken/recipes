Pie Crust
=========

Makes two 20 cm pie crusts.

Ingredients
-----------
* 300 g flour
* 5 mL salt
* 150 g shortening or lard
* 50 mL coconut oil
* 90 mL water

Directions
----------
1. Sift flour and salt into a bowl.
1. Cut in shortening and coconut oil.
1. Bind together with water.
1. Freeze or refrigerate until use.
